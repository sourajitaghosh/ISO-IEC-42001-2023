üìã Clause 7 Documentation Summary

7.1 Resources ?

Determine and provide resources needed for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving the AI management system.

7.2 Competence ?

Ensure persons doing work under the organization's control have appropriate competence on the basis of education, training, or experience.

7.3 Awareness ?

Persons doing work shall be aware of the AI policy, their contribution to the effectiveness of the AI management system, and the implications of not conforming.

7.4 Communication ?

Determine internal and external communications relevant to the AI management system, including what, when, with whom, and how to communicate.

7.5 Documented Information ?

The AI management system shall include documented information required by ISO 42001 and determined as necessary by the organization for effectiveness.
