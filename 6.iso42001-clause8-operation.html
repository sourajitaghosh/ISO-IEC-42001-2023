8.1 Operational Planning and Control

The organization shall plan, implement, and control the processes needed to meet requirements and to implement the actions determined in risk assessment and treatment. This includes establishing criteria for processes, implementing control of processes, and maintaining documented information.

"The organization shall establish, implement and maintain a process for criteria to evaluate and control AI systems and AI system processes throughout their lifecycle." ‚Äî ISO 42001:2023, Clause 8.1

Implementation at RetailAI Co: Comprehensive operational procedures established for inventory prediction AI system. Lifecycle management includes design validation, deployment checklists, monitoring protocols, and retirement planning.

8.2 AI Risk Assessment

The organization shall establish, implement, and maintain processes to identify and assess risks arising from AI systems. Risk assessments shall be performed at defined intervals and when significant changes occur.

"Risk assessment shall identify risks to interested parties and consider the context of the AI system including its intended purpose, data characteristics, and deployment environment." ‚Äî ISO 42001:2023, Clause 8.2

Current Risk Assessment: Supply chain disruption risk (RISK-2025-087) evaluated as High priority (3√ó4 matrix position). Likelihood rated 3/5 (Possible), Impact rated 4/5 (Major). Comprehensive treatment plan includes redundant model architecture and enhanced monitoring.

8.3 Risk Treatment

The organization shall implement risk treatment plans to address identified risks. Treatment options include risk avoidance, risk reduction, risk sharing, or risk retention. Each treatment shall be documented with assigned responsibilities and timelines.

"The organization shall select risk treatment options considering the effectiveness of controls, feasibility of implementation, and residual risk acceptance criteria." ‚Äî ISO 42001:2023, Clause 8.3

Active Treatment: Redundant prediction models deployed with automatic failover capability. Treatment effectiveness currently 75%, targeting 90% within 45 days. Backup model maintains 86% accuracy during primary system failures.

8.4 Impact Assessment

The organization shall assess the potential impacts of AI systems on individuals, society, and the environment. Impact assessments shall consider fairness, transparency, accountability, safety, and sustainability throughout the AI lifecycle.

"Impact assessment shall evaluate potential benefits and harms across multiple dimensions including ethical, social, environmental, and economic factors." ‚Äî ISO 42001:2023, Clause 8.4
