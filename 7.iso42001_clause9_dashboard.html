Clause 9: Performance Evaluation

Monitor, audit, and review AI Management System performance

üìñ Clause 9 Overview ?
Performance evaluation ensures the AI management system operates effectively and achieves intended outcomes. It encompasses continuous monitoring of key metrics, systematic internal audits to verify compliance, and periodic management reviews to assess strategic alignment and improvement opportunities.
9.1 Monitoring, Measurement, Analysis and Evaluation
Organizations must determine what needs to be monitored and measured, establish methods ensuring valid results, define when monitoring occurs, and identify who analyzes and evaluates results. This includes tracking AI system performance, accuracy metrics, operational objectives, and risk indicators.
9.2 Internal Audit
Conduct planned internal audits at regular intervals to verify the AI management system conforms to ISO 42001 requirements and is effectively implemented and maintained. Audit programs must consider the importance of processes, changes affecting the organization, and results of previous audits.
9.3 Management Review
Top management must review the AI management system at planned intervals to ensure continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness, and alignment with strategic direction. Reviews consider status of actions from previous reviews, changes in context, performance information, and opportunities for improvement.
