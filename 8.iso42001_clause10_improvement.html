üìö ISO 42001 Clause 10 - Key Requirements

10.1 Continual Improvement

"The organization shall continually improve the suitability, adequacy and effectiveness of the AI management system."

Organizations must systematically enhance their AI management system by considering results from monitoring, evaluation, analysis, audits, and management reviews. This includes identifying opportunities for improvement and implementing necessary actions.

10.2 Nonconformity and Corrective Action

"When a nonconformity occurs, the organization shall: react to the nonconformity, evaluate the need for action, implement any action needed, review the effectiveness of corrective action, and update risks and opportunities if necessary."

Systematic approach to identifying, documenting, and resolving issues ensures continuous system maturity and compliance maintenance.
